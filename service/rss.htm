  • What is RSS?
    RSS is an XML-based format for sharing and distributing Web content such as news headlines. Using an RSS reader, you can view data feeds from various news sources including headlines, summaries, and links to full stories.

    How do I access RSS?
    RSS/News Aggregators (also called Readers) will download and display RSS feeds for you. A number of free and commercial News Aggregators are available for download.
