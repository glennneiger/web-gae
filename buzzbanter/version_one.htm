Before you know you need it.
The Buzz is a unique application that will automatically update with each new post and bring you value added information all day directly to your desktop. There are in depth conversations and actionable ideas each day. Topics include a wide variety of investments from equities to derivatives, to commodities to bonds. It's insight you won't get anywhere else.

Who's it for?
Independent traders, money managers, anyone who is interested in the stock market and wants to benefit from the ideas, perspective and astute observations of Minyanville's trusted contributors. The Buzz acts as your own independent trading desk, sharing ideas and knowledge.
    Specifically you'll get:
  • Long and short term investment ideas.
  • Analysis of important news of the day along with macro themes that will affect your portfolio.
  • A mix of fundamental analysis and technical analysis.
  • Insight and Ideas from those who know the markets best.

Some of our contributors include: Todd Harrison, Jeff Cooper, Kevin Depew, Smita Sadana, Peter Atwater, Scott Redler, Barry Ritholtz and many more!
