  • Why Minyanville.com?

    Minyanville creates content and provides tools to help investors of all ages. Our mission is to effect positive change through financial understanding.

    Minyanville.com attracts 1.5 million unique visitors and 10 million page views a month. We create, produce and curate branded market commentary and Emmy Award winning video to inform, educate and entertain people about the world of investing, business and finance.

    Content is distributed directly on Minyanville.com and through licensing and syndication agreements with major industry partners such as Yahoo!, MarketWatch, AOL, MSN, Thestreet.com, TD Ameritrade, E*Trade and other leading destinations.

    Sponsorships and Direct Ads

    Minyanville works with advertisers through its exclusive representative, Investing Media Solutions (IMS), to craft strategies that engage prospects and clients. In addition to standard advertising units, Minyanville is expert at creating custom-made opportunities in a range of formats and platforms designed to meet marketers' communication needs. Surrounded by world class content, either regular features or those created exclusively for an advertiser, Minyanville has proven success integrating marketers' messages into content and creating an environment where quality prospects can socialize and share.
