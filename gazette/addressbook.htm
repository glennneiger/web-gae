How to Add Minyanville to Your Address Book

To ensure you receive emails from Minyanville, please add support@minyanville.com to your address book now.


Many corporations and commerical email providers now employ filtering to block unsolicited commercial email (spam). These systems often incorrectly flag newsletters and other subscription-based messages as spam, and redirect these messages to a "bulk" or "trash" folder.


The directions below will help you configure most popular email programs and web mail providers to ensure that you receive all of our emails. Please consult your mail program's help section or your ISP if your questions cannot be answered here.


Microsoft Outlook 2003 | Microsoft Outlook XP |Micorosft Outlook Express | AOL | Earthlink | Gmail | MSN Hotmail | Lotus Notes | Netscape/Mozilla/Thunderbird | Netscape Mail | Yahoo! Mail

  1. Open the Minyanville email.
  2. Right click the from address (support@minyanville.com).
  3. Click "Add Sender to Senders Safe List" in the drop-down dialog box.

Outlook XP/2002

  1. Open the Minyanville email.
  2. Right click the from address (support@minyanville.com).
  3. Click "Add Sender to Contact" in the drop-down dialog box.

Outlook Express

  1. Right-click the email from Minyanville.
  2. Click "Add to Address Book" on the shortcut menu

  1. Open the Minyanville email.
  2. Click on the "Add Address" icon.
  3. The Minyanville email address is automatically populated in the name and email fields in the "Add Contact" dialogue box. Verify that the information is correct.
  4. Click the Save button.

Lotus Notes

  1. Open the Minyanville email.
  2. Select the Actions Menu
  3. Select Mail Tools
  4. Select "Add Sender to Address Book"

Netscape/Mozilla/Thunderbird

  1. Open the Minyanville email.
  2. In the From field, right-click the email address (support@minyanville.com).
  3. Click "Add to Address Book" on the shortcut menu.
  4. Click the "OK" button.

Netscape Webmail

  1. Open the Minyanville email.
  2. Click on the email address (support@minyanville.com) in the From field
  3. Click "Add to Address Book" on the popup
  4. Click the "OK" button.
